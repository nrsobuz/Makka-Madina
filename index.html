Garment CAD Software

Crea is a Italian 2D CAD Software fully integrate with 3D CAD Lotta & Vstitcher. It is the most advanced new generation system to create and modify pattern models and also to develop sizes. It’s continuously updated by our R&D team, based in the headquarter in Italy, and this is why it has the most advanced and user-friendly Windows interface. With Create many functions are done automatically to ensure rapid design and high quality of work, with the registry tools you can quickly select names and entities, quickly rename the pieces and manage the history of the patterns (opening and saving intermediate sessions , with previous work recovery). It is also easy to create size charts, generate reports and thumbnails, export to PDF.
